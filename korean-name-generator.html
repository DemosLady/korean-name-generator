🌸 What Is a Korean Name?

Korean names consist of a family name (성, seong) and a given name (이름, ireum). The given name usually has two syllables, each carrying meaning through Hanja characters — like 지혜 (ji-hye, "wisdom") or 민준 (min-jun, "clever and handsome").
